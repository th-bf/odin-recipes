Lemon Cookies

Description

This is the recipe for some fabulous lemon cookies. Please don't mess it up.

Ingredients

  • Sugar;
  • Lemon (not a leon);
  • An oven.

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar with the lemon (not the leon);
  2. Smash it in the oven for 15 min;
  3. Get inside the oven and eat it from there.
